About me

I paint in an intuitive process. Sometimes it is the raw material, coincidence, that get a painting started, sometimes it is observations that I interpret.

Oftentimes, a painting will undergo several iterations before it arrives at its final destination. I like to get lost, only to find an unexpected path out of the forest.

Born and raised in Southern Germany, I studied cultural anthropology in Heidelberg and Freiburg i.Br. Additionally, I spent some time in Indonesia and Romania.
